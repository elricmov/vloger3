STORIE OF THE ANIME


JUJUTSU KAISEN

is a popular Japanese anime based on the manga written by Gege Akutami. It follows the story of Yuji Itadori, a high school student who becomes involved in the world of sorcery after he accidentally swallows a cursed object, the finger of a powerful cursed spirit named Sukuna. As a result, Yuji becomes the host for Sukuna, the "King of Curses," and gains extraordinary powers, though it puts his life at constant risk. The anime blends supernatural elements, intense battles, and deep emotional themes. The world of Jujutsu Kaisen is filled with "Cursed Spirits" that feed on negative human emotions, and the Jujutsu Sorcerers are tasked with protecting the world by exorcising these dangerous curses. Yuji, alongside fellow sorcerers like Megumi Fushiguro, Nobara Kugisaki, and his mentor Satoru Gojo, battles curses while navigating personal dilemmas and uncovering dark secrets within the sorcerer society. The series is praised for its dynamic animation, especially its fight scenes, along with its compelling characters, dark humor, and exploration of life, death, and moral complexity. Jujutsu Kaisen has become one of the top modern shonen anime since its debut in 2020.

ITADURI YUJI

ROLE:

Protagonist

ABILITIES :

Superhuman strength, host of Ryomen Sukuna

DESCRIPTION :

Yuji Itadori is a high school student with extraordinary physical abilities. After swallowing a cursed object (Sukuna's finger),
he becomes the vessel of Sukuna, the King of Curses. Despite the danger, Yuji retains his kind-hearted nature and joins
Jujutsu Sorcerers to fight against curses and protect people.

Megumi Fushiguro

ROLE:

Supporting Character, Jujutsu Sorcerer

ABILITIES :

Shikigami summoning, Ten Shadows Technique

DESCRIPTION :

Megumi is a reserved but skilled Jujutsu Sorcerer
who can summon shikigami, spiritual familiars used in battle.
As a first-year student at Tokyo Jujutsu High, he acts as Yuji's ally and friend,
though he is deeply concerned about morality and justice.

Nobara Kugisaki

ROLE:

Supporting Character, Jujutsu Sorcerer

ABILITIES :

Straw Doll Technique, Resonance, Hairpin

DESCRIPTION :

Nobara is a confident and strong-willed
sorcerer. Using her hammer, nails,
and a straw doll, she can exorcise curses
with deadly precision.
She’s not only determined to succeed but
also cares deeply about her comrades.

Satoru Gojo

ROLE:

Mentor,Strongest Jujutsu Sorcere

ABILITIES :

Limitless, Six Eyes, Infinity

DESCRIPTION :

Gojo is one of the most powerful sorcerers in the world.
He is the mentor of Yuji, Megumi, and Nobara at Tokyo Jujutsu High.
With his ability to manipulate space, time, and reality through his Limitless Cursed Technique,
Gojo can make himself untouchable.
His laid-back and humorous nature contrasts with his overwhelming strength.

Ryomen Sukuna

ROLE:

Antagonist, Cursed Spirit

ABILITIES :

Dismantle, Domain Expansion: Malevolent Shrine

DESCRIPTION :

Sukuna is a centuries-old Cursed Spirit known as the King of Curses.
He resides within Yuji's body after Yuji consumes one of his fingers. Sukuna is sadistic, powerful, and terrifying,
with ambitions to regain his full power and reign over the curse world.

Maki Zenin

ROLE:

Jujutsu Sorcerer, Zenin Clan Member

ABILITIES :

Exceptional physical strength, cursed tools expert

DESCRIPTION :

Maki is a second-year student at Tokyo
Jujutsu High
and a member of the prestigious Zenin clan,
though she lacks the cursed energy her family
is known for.
Despite this,
Maki compensates with her incredible
physical powerss and mastery of cursed weapons.

Panda

ROLE:

Jujutsu Sorcerer, Cursed Corpser

ABILITIES :

Gorilla Mode, Balanced Mode

DESCRIPTION :

Panda is an intelligent, talking cursed corpse created by Jujutsu High's principal,
Masamichi Yaga. Panda has three cores—Panda, Gorilla,
and a third undisclosed one—that allow him to switch between different forms and fighting styles.

Toge Inumaki

ROLE:

Jujutsu Sorcerer, Second-Year Student

ABILITIES :

Cursed Speech

DESCRIPTION :

Toge is a quiet second-year sorcerer whose powers revolve around his ability
to use cursed words to manipulate others.
By speaking specific words, Toge can force his opponents to obey his commands. Because of his power,
he often speaks in food-based words to avoid accidentally harming others.

Yuta Okkotsu

ROLE:

Protagonist of Jujutsu Kaisen 0,
Special Grade Sorcerer

ABILITIES :

Rika, Reverse Cursed Technique

DESCRIPTION :

Yuta is the protagonist of
Jujutsu Kaisen 0 and a special-grade
sorcerer with enormous potential.
He is haunted by the cursed spirit of
his childhood friend, Rika,
but gradually learns
to harness her power.
Yuta is both kind-hearted
and immensely powerful.

Kento Nanami

ROLE:

Jujutsu Sorcerer, Former Salaryman

ABILITIES :

Ratio Technique, Overtime

DESCRIPTION :

Nanami is a serious and practical sorcerer who
worked as a salaryman before joining Jujutsu High. He views exorcising curses as
just another job but is extremely reliable and skilled in battle.
His "Ratio Technique" allows him to find weak points on any target.

Boogie Woogie (Teleportation technique)
